IPM 2022/23 - Group 7

About

The goal of the project is to establish an app that increases international student’s social interaction, by means of combining all the differnt platforms in one app. The app will provide an insight in all activties organised by different parties (e.ESN, ELL, university). Additionaly, the app has the option to join groups with students having similair interests (e.g. surfing, party’s, student association).
